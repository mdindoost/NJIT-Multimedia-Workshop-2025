NJIT Multimedia Intelligence Workshop Series (MMI)

An annual research workshop at NJIT bringing together students, international researchers, and practitioners working at the intersection of multimedia, AI, and multimodal learning.

MMI is a community-driven initiative with the long-term vision of evolving into an international conference on Multimedia Intelligence.
